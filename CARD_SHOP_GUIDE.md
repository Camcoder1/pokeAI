## ğŸ¯ Shopping Strategy

### When to Buy Singles:

**Good Deal if:**
- Card shop price < Market price shown
- Card has "Chase Card!" or "High Value!" badge
- You're completing a collection

**Skip if:**
- Card shop price > 110% of market price
- Card is under $5 (unless you need it)
- Pull rate is common (better to pull from packs)

### When to Buy Sealed:

Use the main analysis to decide:
- **OPEN** - If EV significantly exceeds box price
- **HOLD SEALED** - If sealed appreciating
- **RESELL** - If sealed price inflated

## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### 1. **Check Condition**
- Market prices assume Near Mint (NM)
- Heavily played cards worth 50-70% less
- Always inspect cards before buying

### 2. **Negotiate**
- Show shop owner the market price
- Ask for bulk discount on multiple cards
- Trade-in value usually 60-70% of market

### 3. **Timing**
- New sets have higher prices initially
- Prices stabilize 2-3 months after release
- Buy singles after initial hype dies down

### 4. **Set Number Lookup**
- Use set numbers to find cards quickly
- Match against shop binders
- Verify you're looking at correct printing

### 5. **Pull Rate Context**
- "1 in 36 packs" = Average 1 per box
- "1 in 6 packs" = Average 6 per box
- Use this to decide if opening is worth it
